BIOGRAPHY

I’m Rafael Borges, a Full Stack Developer.

My curiosity for technology started early ,taking apart videogames and fixing anything I could.
At fourteen years old, I was already working in a toner and printer laboratory, learning everything through hands-on practice.

Before entering the technology area at thirty years old,
I went through different jobs that gave me a practical and human understanding of how real people use systems.

Today I build responsive websites, landing pages, and practical solutions, always focused on simplicity, usability, and user experience.
I am also studying backend development, mobile development, software architecture, and improving my English to grow even further.

I believe that every business, whether small, medium, or large, deserves accessible and efficient technology to achieve new results, and I am ready to make it happen.
